About 📱

What lies under this app? 🤔

Say Hi to the Effortless TODO app, your productivity companion 😚

The app with zero effort! Just keep adding your daily routine tasks and in the end of the day it will archive all the previous day's tasks automatically ☑️

Drag n Drop to play around with the tasks 👆🏻

With Angular 16 at its core, this app delivers a seamless user experience 👨🏻‍💻

PWA Ready, installable on Android and iOS 📲

Your tasks are safely stored using local storage, always ready for you 📂

p.s. do not clear your browser storage! all the tasks will be cleared 🚫
